Narwhal Tutoring Terms of Service

Thank you for choosing Narwhal Tutoring! Before you proceed, please review our Terms of Service carefully.

Late Booking Policy:

Clients must confirm tutor availability for lesson bookings made within a week of today.

Cancellation Policy:
  • Proper Notice (> 2 hours): Tutor and student should collaborate to find a suitable time for a make-up lesson.
  • Late Notice: No make-up lesson owed.

Booking Policy:
  • Client books a lesson and makes payment.
  • Client contacts the tutor with the lesson location.
  • Tutor confirms availability for the session.
  • If the tutor cannot make the session, a reschedule or refund will be provided.

Refund Policy:

For refund inquiries, please contact Noah West at 0421 286 031.

Travel Fees Policy:
  • Tutors will drive to any location within 10km of their suburb unless otherwise specified in the tutor's description.
  • Additional travel fees may apply if the distance is challenging for the tutor.

Multiple Students Policy:

For multiple students attending the same lesson, an extra $30 per hour will be added to the cost of the lesson per student.
